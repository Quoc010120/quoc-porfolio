Data Visualization Project

  • Insights of wildlife impacts

    This Tableau project offers an insightful look into the wildlife strikes that affected UN airlines from 2000 to 2015. Using data from the FAA Wildlife Database, I visualized key statistics, trends, and patterns of wildlife impacts on flights, revealing how certain species and regions have contributed to these incidents over time.

  • Trees and money in New York

    In this intriguing Tableau project, I explored the relationship between nature and wealth in New York City. Using data on NYC tree and median household income, I created an interactive dashboard to uncover how tree distribution correlates with income levels across neighborhoods. This project offers unique insights into urban greenery and socioeconomic patterns, showing how environmental factors intersect with financial well-being.
